FAQS

Our FAQ section is here to give you all the information you need to feel confident in your choice. Whether you're curious about the Lifetime Certification, our commission rates, or how our marketing support works, you'll find detailed explanations that clarify how Bite can help you take control of your orders and profits.

How Do I Receive Payments?

Once a customer places an order through Bite, payments are processed securely via Stripe. The funds are deposited directly into your bank account after processing, giving you quick and easy access to your earnings.

How Does Stripe Work?

Stripe is our secure payment processor, handling all transactions from Bite orders. Stripe processes each payment from your customers and then deposits the funds directly into your account, minus Stripe's transaction fee (usually 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction). It’s a reliable, widely trusted platform to ensure your payments are handled smoothly.

How Does The DoorDash API Work?

Bite integrates with DoorDash’s API, allowing you to offer seamless delivery services without additional setup. When a customer orders for delivery, the DoorDash API connects directly with DoorDash’s fleet of drivers to handle the delivery, saving you time and resources in managing deliveries yourself.

How Much Does DoorDash Charge For Their Delivery API?

The delivery fees vary based on factors like order distance and demand, and are typically passed on to the customer as a delivery fee. In some cases, restaurants may cover part of the cost to offer discounts or free delivery as a promotion. Bite ensures full transparency so you’re aware of any associated delivery fees.

What Kind Of Support Is Included With Bite?

Bite includes comprehensive support to make sure you’re always set up for success. From onboarding assistance and menu setup to ongoing troubleshooting and updates, our team is here to help. You can reach us via email, phone, or through our support chat for any assistance you may need.

Can I Cancel Or Switch Plans?

Yes, you can cancel or switch plans at any time. We understand that flexibility is important for restaurant owners, so we’ve made it easy to adjust your plan as your business needs change. Just reach out to our support team, and we’ll help you make any changes.
